Custom CRM That Increased Lead Conversion by 42%

We developed a custom CRM solution that centralized lead management, automated follow-ups, and integrated multiple lead sources, helping a growing B2B business improve sales efficiency and customer engagement.

THE CHALLENGE

Fragmented leads, lost revenue.

The client managed leads across IndiaMART, Justdial, WhatsApp, website forms, emails, and spreadsheets. This resulted in missed follow-ups, duplicate records, and poor sales visibility.

Scattered Lead Sources

Leads came from multiple platforms without centralization.

Delayed Follow-ups

Manual tracking led to slow responses and lost opportunities.

No Centralized Data

Customer information was stored in Excel files and emails.

Poor Sales Visibility

No real-time insights into pipeline, team performance, or revenue.

OUR SOLUTION

A bespoke, unified database.

We built a custom CRM platform tailored to the client's sales process and business workflow.

Lead Management

Complete contact profiles linked to communication logs.

B2B Directory Integrations

Real-time webhook ingestion from IndiaMART and Justdial.

Pipeline Tracking

Visual Kanban pipelines with conversion rates per deal stage.

Email Integration

Auto-sync conversation history under relevant client records.

Follow-up Automation

Scheduled SMS, email, and task reminders for sales reps.

Dashboard Analytics

Real-time charts showing conversion rates, revenue, and rep logs.

WhatsApp Integration

Send automated notifications and chat logs straight to the deal.

Role-Based Access

Secure data boundaries for sales reps, managers, and executives.

BUSINESS IMPACT

Streamlined sales, real metrics.

The custom CRM streamlined sales operations by automating lead management, centralizing customer data, and providing real-time sales insights.
