§ 06 · METHOD

From idea to launch,
four steps.

Same disciplined process for every project we take on. It's how we stay on time, on budget, and worth the price tag.

01

Listen.

A few honest conversations. What do you need? Who uses it? Then we figure out the smallest first version worth building.

02

Design.

Screens, data flows, contracts. You see how it'll look and how it'll work before we write a line of code.

03

Build.

Two weeks at a time. At the end of every two, real working software, decide what's right, and we adjust.

04

Stay around.

Going live is the start, not the end. We watch, fix, and grow it with you. No disappearing after launch.
