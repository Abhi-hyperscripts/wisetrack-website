§ 01

What's a cookie.

A cookie is a small text file a website saves on your device. Cookies that come from the site you're visiting are first-party; cookies set by external services embedded on the page are third-party. wisetrack.in sets only first-party cookies.

§ 03

Analytics.

We use a privacy-preserving analytics tool that does not set third-party cookies, does not fingerprint visitors, and stores only aggregate metrics — page views, referrer, country (from IP, discarded after lookup). We do not track individual users across sessions.

§ 04

Third parties.

wisetrack.in does not embed Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, LinkedIn Insight, X/Twitter trackers, TikTok pixels, or any other ad-network code. Fonts are served from Google Fonts (Google may receive your IP for that purpose only) and our scripts come from JS CDNs (Tailwind Play CDN). No cookies are set by these requests.

§ 05

How to opt out.

You can refuse non-essential cookies via your browser settings. All modern browsers expose a per-site cookie manager. Refusing essential cookies will break forms and preference persistence but won't stop you from reading the site.
