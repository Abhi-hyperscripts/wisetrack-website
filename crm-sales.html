§ Support & Info

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CRM software?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) software helps businesses manage leads, customers, sales pipelines, communication, and follow-ups from one centralized platform.

Is Ragenaizer CRM suitable for small businesses?

Yes. It is fully scalable and designed with customizable features so that small businesses can start easily and unlock advanced automation as they grow.

Can I customize the CRM?

Absolutely. You can customize fields, pipelines, stages, notification triggers, and user access roles to align exactly with your existing workflows.

Does Ragenaizer support integrations?

Yes. It integrates natively with email (SMTP/IMAP), WhatsApp business API, website forms, Facebook Lead Ads, Google Ads, and offers custom webhook triggers.

Is my business data secure?

Yes. We enforce bank-grade SSL encryption, multi-tenant database isolation, row-level security policies (RLS), and perform automated redundant backups daily.

Do you provide implementation and support?

Yes. We offer standard onboarding assistance, documentation, and dedicated enterprise support plans to guide your team through setup and data migrations.
